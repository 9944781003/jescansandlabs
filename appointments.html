Services

  • Cardiology clinic

    The clinic is staffed by a team of highly-trained cardiologists, and technicians. We offer various non-invasive, diagnostic testing to all of our patients, such as electrocardiograms (ECG's), echocardiograms (echoes), exercise-stress testing, and stress-echocardiograms.

  • Orthopaedic clinic

    Here at our orthopedic Center, we have medical specialty that focuses on injuries and diseases of your body's musculoskeletal system. This complex system includes your bones, joints, ligaments, tendons, muscles, and nerves and allows you to move, work, and be active.

  • Paediatric

    Our Paediatric Outpatient Clinic offers specialized pediatric care to our youngest patients and their families - ensuring they receive access to high quality patient care close to home. The clinic is led by a specialized inter-professional pediatric team.

  • Dermatology clinic

    Our Dermatology department has provide a high standard of individual care to all our patients. Clear advice and information on skin health issues will be given to our patients, waiting times will be kept to a minimum.

  • Endocrinology clinic

    The Section of Adult and Pediatric Endocrinology, Diabetes and Metabolism offers outpatient diagnostic and treatment programs, and an endocrine hormone assay laboratory that performs a range of tests.

  • ENT

    This ENT clinic offers services to patients suffering from ear, nose, neck and throat ailments as well as hearing loss, dizziness and tinnitus. ENT OP also offers a surgical hearing restoration service which include the cochlear implant, programmed by doctors.

  • Obstetrics & Gynecology

    The Obstetrics & Gynaecology Centre (O&G) at the JE Multispecialty clinic delivers a full range of sub-specialties within the obstetrics and gynaecology practice, dedicated to the growing health needs of women, from puberty to after menopause.

  • Radiology

    Our Radiology Department provides a full array of imaging procedures to support accurate, timely diagnosis and treatment. State-of-the-art facilities and technology allow for a wide variety of services, from basic x-ray studies to sophisticated, real-time imaging of the heart.

  • Urology

    Our Urology Clinic provides the prevention, early detection, diagnosis, treatment and rehabilitation of benign and malignant tumors, infections, stone diseases, and malfunctions within the field of urology.

  • Computerised ECG

    ECG is the process of recording the electrical activity of the heart over a period of time using electrodes placed on a patient's body. An electrocardiogram is a painless, noninvasive way to diagnose many common types of heart problems.

  • TMT

    TMT is the most widely used test in the diagnosis of ischemic heart disease. It involves recording the 12-lead ECG before, during, and after exercise on a treadmill. A cardiac stress test is a recording of the heart's activity during exercise.

  • PFT

    Pulmonary function tests (PFTs) are a group of breathing tests. They can show how well your lungs are working. PFTs may measure how much air you can blow out, how much air your lungs can hold at different times, how fast you are blowing air out.

  • Digital X - Ray

    Radiology Associates offers the full range of diagnostic x-ray studies including chest, musculoskeletal, abdominal, gastrointestinal and genitourinary x-ray studies. The studies are performed in a digital format utilizing our advanced Fuji CR system.

  • Ultrasound

    Comprehensive ultrasound services including routine abdominal, pelvic, obstetrical, thyroid, and vascular studies are provided utilizing state-of-the-art ATL ultrasound equipment. The service specializes in the performance of ultrasound guided biopsies.

  • Colour Doppler

    A Doppler ultrasound is a test that uses sound waves to examine parts of the body. Doppler ultrasound is used to View the flow of blood through vessels and/or the heart, View and evaluate blockages to blood flow, such as clots, Monitor blood flow through repaired blood vessels such as bypass grafts, Examine a baby during a pregnancy.

  • Echocardiography

    Our echocardiography laboratory performs extensively detailed quantitative studies on state of the art equipment. We specialize in quantification of valvular heart disease, diastolic dysfunction, and direct side-by-side digital image comparison of sequential studies.

  • Physiotherapy

    Physiotherapists help people affected by injury, illness or disability through movement and exercise, manual therapy, education and advice. They maintain health for people of all ages, helping patients to manage pain and prevent disease.

  • General surgery

    General surgery is a surgical specialty that focuses on abdominal contents including esophagus, stomach, small bowel, colon, liver, pancreas, gallbladder and bile ducts, and often the thyroid gland.

  • General Medicine

    General medicine is the medical specialty dealing with the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult diseases. Physicians specializing in internal medicine are called internists, or physicians.
